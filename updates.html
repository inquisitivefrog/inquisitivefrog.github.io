Initial release

DinoGames launches with 36 educational mini-games for prehistoric life enthusiasts.

  • 12 dinosaur mini-games
  • 12 pterosaur mini-games
  • 12 marine reptile mini-games

Activities include fact matching, image puzzles, animal comparisons, spoken names, formations, habitats, diets, flora, fauna, footprints, eggs, teeth, and other paleontology topics.

DinoGames works without accounts, advertisements, analytics, in-app purchases, or an Internet connection during gameplay.
