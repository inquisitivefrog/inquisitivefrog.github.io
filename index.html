DinoGames

Explore prehistoric life through fun, interactive educational mini-games.

DinoGames introduces players to dinosaurs, pterosaurs, and marine reptiles through matching games, puzzles, and comparison challenges that make learning both engaging and memorable.

Version 1.0 Features

  • 36 educational mini-games
  • 12 Dinosaur games
  • 12 Pterosaur games
  • 12 Marine Reptile games
  • Audio pronunciation for names and educational topics
  • Offline gameplay — no Internet connection required after installation
  • No advertising
  • No in-app purchases

Whether you're discovering your first dinosaur or expanding your knowledge of prehistoric life, DinoGames combines learning with playful challenges and colorful animations.
