DinoGames Privacy Policy

```

Effective date: July 2026

DinoGames is an educational game for dinosaur, pterosaur, and marine reptile enthusiasts. It was designed to work without advertising, accounts, tracking, analytics, in-app purchases, or an Internet connection during gameplay.

Information Collection

DinoGames does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not require a user account or sign-in and does not communicate with an external server during normal gameplay.

Progress Stored on the Device

DinoGames includes a Track My Progress feature. The app may save game progress locally on the device so that a player can continue after leaving the app, losing battery power, or having a game interrupted.

This progress information remains on the device. It is not transmitted to the developer or to a third-party service.

DinoGames does not currently create separate player accounts or profiles for multiple children using the same device.

Children's Privacy

DinoGames is designed so that children can play without providing personal information.

The app does not request or collect names, email addresses, locations, contacts, photographs, camera images, microphone recordings, or other personal information.

Advertising, Analytics, and Purchases

DinoGames does not display advertisements.

DinoGames does not use third-party analytics, behavioral tracking, or advertising networks.

DinoGames does not include in-app purchases.

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of DinoGames change in a future version, this policy will be updated on this page.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent to dinogames.dinosupport@gmail.com .
```
