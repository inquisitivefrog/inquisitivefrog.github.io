When Reporting a Problem

Please include, if possible:

  • Your iPhone or iPad model
  • Your iOS version
  • The name of the mini-game you were playing
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • A screenshot, if available

Educational Content

DinoGames is intended to make learning about prehistoric life fun and engaging. If you believe information about a dinosaur, pterosaur, marine reptile, geological formation, habitat, flora, fauna, footprint, egg, or other prehistoric topic is inaccurate or could be improved, please let us know. Scientific knowledge continues to evolve, and thoughtful feedback is welcome.
